# 7-Day Japan Itinerary: Tokyo, Seaside, Mountains & History

**Goal:** Hike-worthy mountains, a historic town, and a seaside town — with minimal transit time.

---

## Recommended Route

```
Tokyo (1–2)  →  Kamakura (3–4)  →  Hakone (5–6)  →  Tokyo (7)
```

**Total transit time across all legs: ~4 hours.**

---

## Day 1–2: Tokyo

**Arrival transport:**

| Airport | Train | To | Time | Cost |
|---------|-------|----|------|------|
| Narita (NRT) | JR Narita Express (N'EX) | Tokyo Station | ~55 min | ~3,070 JPY |
| Narita (NRT) | Keisei Skyliner | Nippori / Ueno | ~40 min | ~2,570 JPY |
| Haneda (HND) | Tokyo Monorail | Hamamatsucho | ~15 min | ~500 JPY |
| Haneda (HND) | Keikyu Line | Shinagawa | ~15 min | ~300 JPY |

**Getting around Tokyo:** JR Yamanote Line (loop line) + Tokyo Metro 24-hour ticket (~600–800 JPY).

**Suggested:** Explore a neighbourhood like Asakusa (Senso-ji temple), Shinjuku, Shibuya, or Ueno Park.

---

## Day 3–4: Kamakura & Enoshima (Historic + Seaside)

### Day 3: Tokyo → Kamakura

| Route | From | To | Train | Time | Cost |
|-------|------|----|-------|------|------|
| A | Tokyo Station | Kamakura Station | JR Yokosuka Line (direct) | ~60 min | 950 JPY |
| B | Shinjuku Station | Kamakura Station | JR Shonan-Shinjuku Line (direct) | ~60 min | 950 JPY |
| C | Shinagawa Station | Kamakura Station | JR Yokosuka Line (direct) | ~50 min | 800 JPY |

**Covered by the JR Pass if you have one.**

### Getting around Kamakura/Enoshima:

- **Enoden (Enoshima Electric Railway):** Runs between Kamakura Station and Fujisawa Station, passing through the coast. Stops at Hase (Great Buddha), Enoshima, and the beach.
- Cost: ~160–310 JPY per ride, or Enoden 1-day pass (~800 JPY).
- **Walking:** Kamakura's temples (Tsurugaoka Hachimangu, Hokokuji bamboo temple) are walkable from Kamakura Station.
- **Enoshima Island:** Walk across the bridge from Enoshima Station, or take the Enoshima Escar (outdoor escalator, ~360 JPY).

### Day 4 highlights:
- Morning: Great Buddha (Kotoku-in) at Hase Station
- Lunch: Komachi-dori shopping street
- Afternoon: Enoshima Island — caves, shrine, lighthouse, sunset over Mt. Fuji (weather permitting)
- Evening: Beach / seaside dinner

## Day 5–6: Hakone (Mountains, Hiking, Onsen)

### Day 5: Kamakura → Hakone

| Leg | From | To | Train | Time |
|-----|------|----|-------|------|
| 1 | Kamakura Station | Ofuna Station | JR Yokosuka Line / JR Shonan-Shinjuku Line | ~8 min |
| 2 | Ofuna Station | Odawara Station | JR Tokaido Line (rapid) | ~25 min |
| 3 | Odawara Station | Hakone-Yumoto Station | Hakone Tozan Railway | ~15 min |

**Total: ~50 min from Kamakura. Approx 1,100 JPY.**

### The Hakone Loop (classic day circuit):

Buy the **Hakone Free Pass (2-day, ~5,000 JPY from Odawara)** — covers all transport below.

| Step | Transport | Route |
|------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Hakone Tozan Railway (cogwheel train) | Hakone-Yumoto → Gora (~40 min) |
| 2 | Hakone Tozan Cable Car | Gora → Sounzan (~10 min) |
| 3 | Hakone Ropeway | Sounzan → Owakudani → Togendai (~30 min) |
| 4 | Pirate Ship (Lake Ashi) | Togendai → Hakone-machi / Moto-Hakone (~30 min) |
| 5 | Hakone Tozan Bus | Moto-Hakone → Hakone-Yumoto (~35 min) |

### Hiking options:

- **Old Tokaido Cedar-lined trail:** From Moto-Hakone to Hakone-Yumoto. Well-preserved section of the Edo-period highway. ~2–3 hours, easy/moderate.
- **Mt. Kintoki:** Take bus to Kintoki-jinja-iriguchi from Hakone-Yumoto. Summit in ~90 min. Panoramic views of Mt. Fuji.
- **Owakudani hiking course:** Short trail around the volcanic valley (if the trail is open — sometimes closed for volcanic activity).

### History:

- Hakone Checkpoint (Sekisho): Restored Edo-period checkpoint on Lake Ashi.
- Amazake-chaya: 400-year-old teahouse on the Old Tokaido trail.

### Onsen:
- Hakone-Yumoto has day-use onsen (Tenzan, ~1,400 JPY entry).
- Many ryokans offer day-visitor baths.

## Day 7: Hakone → Tokyo (Departure)

| Route | From | To | Train | Time | Cost |
|-------|------|----|-------|------|------|
| A (fast) | Hakone-Yumoto → Odawara | Tokyo Station | Hakone Tozan Railway + JR Shinkansen Kodama | ~55 min total | ~3,300 JPY |
| B (direct) | Hakone-Yumoto | Shinjuku | Odakyu Romancecar (limited express) | ~85 min | ~2,330 JPY |
| C (budget) | Hakone-Yumoto | Shinjuku | Odakyu Line (local/express) | ~100 min | ~1,260 JPY |

From Tokyo/Shinjuku, connect to Narita or Haneda for departure.

## Alternate Itinerary: Nikko (Bigger Mountains)

If you want more dramatic mountain scenery and are willing to trade extra transit time:

| Day | Location | Transport |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| 1–2 | Tokyo | As above |
| 3–4 | **Nikko** | JR Tohoku Shinkansen (Tokyo → Utsunomiya, ~50 min) + JR Nikko Line (~45 min). Total ~2 hrs, ~5,600 JPY. Tobu Nikko Line from Asakusa is cheaper (~2 hrs, ~1,400 JPY). |
| 5–6 | **Kamakura** | Nikko → Tokyo (~2 hrs) → Kamakura (~1 hr). Total ~3.5 hrs. |
| 7 | Kamakura → Tokyo | ~1 hr, depart |

Nikko offers: UNESCO Toshogu shrine, Senjogahara marshland hike (~2 hrs), Lake Chuzenji, Kegon Falls. More dramatic but transit-heavy.
